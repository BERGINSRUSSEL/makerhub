About us

MakerHub is a community designed to inspire, enable, challenge, and support makers using various technologies available. We are your one stop destination for all your dicovery needs. We support not only in-house projects done by students, but also the project available from industries and government organisations. We have got a full pledged lab starting with basic hand tools to advanced CNCs. All tools are available to students as well as public.
We also find solutions to various problems in the field.
